# Cryptocurrency Market Analysis Using Power BI

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing cryptocurrency market data to uncover insights related to market capitalization, trading volume, price performance, volatility, and risk distribution. The dashboard was developed using Power BI and provides an interactive platform for exploring cryptocurrency market trends and behavior.

The project combines data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, and business intelligence techniques to transform raw cryptocurrency data into meaningful and actionable insights.

---

## Problem Statement

The cryptocurrency market is highly volatile and generates large volumes of data daily. Raw datasets often contain inconsistencies, missing values, and complex relationships that make analysis challenging.

This project aims to clean, process, and analyze cryptocurrency market data to identify market trends, performance patterns, growth opportunities, and risk factors through interactive visualizations.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze cryptocurrency market capitalization and trading volume.
- Identify top-performing and underperforming cryptocurrencies.
- Evaluate market growth and performance trends.
- Assess cryptocurrency volatility and risk distribution.
- Generate actionable insights through interactive dashboards.

---

## Tools and Technologies Used

- Power BI – Dashboard development and visualization
- Python – Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Microsoft Excel – Initial data inspection and validation
- DAX – KPI calculations and measures

## Dashboard Pages

### Market Overview Dashboard

Provides a high-level overview of the cryptocurrency market through key metrics and performance indicators.

#### Features

- Total Market Capitalization
- Total Market Volume
- Average All-Time High (ATH)
- Number of Capped Coins
- Top Gainers and Top Losers
- Top Cryptocurrencies by Market Capitalization
- Trading Volume Analysis
- Capped vs Uncapped Cryptocurrency Distribution
- Yearly Price Change Analysis

#### Key Insights

- Bitcoin dominates overall market capitalization.
- USDT records the highest trading volume among cryptocurrencies.
- Significant performance differences exist between top gainers and top losers.
- Most cryptocurrencies in the dataset belong to the capped supply category.

---

### Market Performance Dashboard

Analyzes cryptocurrency performance trends and market behavior over time.

#### Features

- Performance Bucket Analysis
- Market Capitalization vs Trading Volume Analysis
- ATH vs Current Price Comparison
- Historical ATH Trend Analysis
- Circulating Supply vs Total Supply Analysis
- Performance Drop Analysis

#### Key Insights

- Many cryptocurrencies are currently trading significantly below their all-time highs.
- High market capitalization does not always indicate strong performance.
- Supply distribution patterns vary considerably across different cryptocurrencies.

---

### Crypto Risk & Opportunity Analysis Dashboard

Evaluates cryptocurrency risk levels, volatility, and growth opportunities.

#### Features

- High Growth Coin Identification
- High Risk Coin Analysis
- Volatility Categorization
- Risk Distribution by Supply Type
- Risk Concentration by Market Capitalization
- Volatility Distribution Analysis
- Supply Utilization Analysis

#### Key Insights

- Small-cap cryptocurrencies exhibit higher levels of risk and volatility.
- High-volatility cryptocurrencies may offer higher growth opportunities but involve greater investment risk.
- Risk exposure differs between capped and uncapped cryptocurrencies.
